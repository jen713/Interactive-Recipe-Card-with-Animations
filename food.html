Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 3/4 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 cup milk

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cream the sugar and butter.
  3. Add the eggs, one at a time.
  4. Sift together flour and baking powder.
  5. Mix the dry ingredients with the milk.
  6. Pour batter into a greased pan and bake for 30-35 minutes.

Preparation Time: 45 minutes

Serves: 8
